Petey's Pizza Panic

Overview

Petey's Pizza Panic is a simple puzzle platforming game developed within the Unity engine for windows devices. The game follows our main character Petey, a slice of pizza, as he attempts to change his fate and escape the pizzeria. Equipped with a cheese grapple and pepperoni shooter gained from his genetic mutations sustained within the baking process, he must face off against opposing mutated toppings to seal his esacpe.

My Role

  • Created physics-based character controller
  • Developed grapple ability
  • Prototyped and designed level 3
  • Rigged character models
  • Animated character models
  • Designed main and pause menu user intefaces
