Evasion Launchapad

Overview

This channel is an ongoing project which I have been running since early 2017. On here I perform covers and remixes of some of my favorite songs on a Launchpad midi device. Accompanied by these performances are lightshows which are developed with various Max for Live plugins. Several videos feature collabortation with other launcpad artists, who I highly suggest you check out as well.

What I Do

  • Cover, edit, and remix tracks within ableton live
  • Program light displays with the use of Max
  • Perform covers on Novation's launchpad
  • Edit and apply effects to videos
  • Design thumbnails and branding for the youtube channel
