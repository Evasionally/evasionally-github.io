Explore - In Development

Overview

A math learning mobile application targeted towards students in developing countries. Within the app users learn the basic mathematical operators through four main games across the solar system: combating alien opponents, breaking through planet atmospheres, racing across planets, and mining for precious resources. Accompanied by a local leaderboard, educators can keep track of their top preforming studnets within the classroom.
